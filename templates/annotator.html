Donald Trump On Gun Violence

Well, let me just tell you before we go any further in Chicago, which has the toughest gun laws in the United States, probably you could say by far, they have more gun violence than any other city. So we have the toughest laws and you have tremendous gun violence.

Chicago has been an epicenter of gun violence and according to FBI data, homicides in that city account for a substantial proportion of a rise in new violent crime. Scholars say that violence there is largely concentrated in a few neighborhoods.

Danielle Kurtzleben NPR Political Reporter

Quoted on September 18, 2015 at the Republican Presidential Debate
